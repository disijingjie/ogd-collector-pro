
=== 政府数据开放平台数据利用绩效评估研究 ===

中文摘要
政府数据开放是数字政府建设的核心内容，亦是数据要素价值化释放的关键路径。然而，我国省级政府数据开放平台普遍存在”开放多、利用少”的结构性悖论——数据目录数量持续增长，但数据有效利用率严重偏低，数据利用绩效的真实状况尚不明朗，制约绩效的关键因素及优化路径亦缺乏系统性实证研究。现有评估体系以供给侧指标为核心，对数据利用效果的测量严重不足，存在”评估范式与政策目标错位”的深层缺陷。
本研究以我国30个省级政府数据开放平台为研究对象，时间跨度为2019—2024年，构建了”4E框架”（Economy供给保障、Efficiency平台效率、Quality数据质量、Effectiveness利用效果），综合运用熵权TOPSIS法、DEA-BCC模型、DEMATEL法、模糊集定性比较分析（fsQCA）和多期双重差分法（Multi-period DID）五种方法，对省级平台数据利用绩效进行系统性测度、诊断与优化路径识别。
研究发现：其一，省级平台综合绩效存在显著区域差异，呈”头部集中、尾部分散”格局，东部沿海省份（浙江、上海、广东、北京、江苏）综合得分均在0.75以上，而后9名省份得分均低于0.50，省际差距显著。其二，“数据口径幻觉”是当前评估体系的系统性偏差，数据目录数量与有效数据集数量的中位数口径比值高达8.2倍，以数据目录数量为基准的现有评估结果存在系统性高估。其三，数据质量是影响数据利用绩效的最关键驱动因素，其中心度（4.23）和原因度（+1.87）均居12个二级指标之首，对利用效果的路径系数（0.47）显著高于供给数量（0.23）。其四，高绩效平台存在三条等效路径：技术驱动型（覆盖度0.312）、需求拉动型（0.287）和制度保障型（0.198），揭示了绩效改善的”等效性”——不同资源禀赋的省份可选择适合自身的优化路径。其五，“数据二十条”配套政策对综合绩效具有显著正向效应（+0.043，p<0.01），但Economy维度效果最大（+0.067），Effectiveness维度效果最小（+0.021），政策对数据利用效果的促进存在明显时滞。
本研究的理论贡献在于：（1）构建了以”效果导向”为核心的4E评估框架，形成从供给导向到效果导向的评估范式跃迁；（2）发现并测量了”数据口径幻觉”现象，揭示了现有评估体系的系统性偏差；（3）将fsQCA与多期DID相结合，形成”宏观因果识别+微观机制揭示”的混合研究设计，为政府数据开放研究提供了新的方法论范式。实践贡献在于：提出了包含国家层面制度重构、平台层面运营升级和区域层面差异化路径的三层次政策建议体系，并制定了2025—2030年三阶段实施路线图。
关键词： 政府数据开放；数据利用绩效；4E框架；fsQCA；多期DID；数据口径幻觉；熵权TOPSIS
Abstract
Open government data (OGD) is a cornerstone of digital government and a critical pathway for realizing data as a factor of production. Provincial-level OGD platforms in China, however, exhibit a structural paradox of “opening much but utilizing little” — catalog counts keep growing while effective data utilization remains critically low. The actual state of data utilization performance remains unclear, and key factors constraining performance and optimization pathways lack systematic empirical investigation. Existing evaluation frameworks are dominated by supply-side indicators, with severely insufficient measurement of data utilization effectiveness, reflecting a deep-seated misalignment between evaluation paradigms and policy objectives.
This study takes 30 provincial-level OGD platforms in China as research objects, spanning 2019 to 2024. A “4E Framework” (供给保障 — supply guarantee, 平台效率 — platform service, 数据质量 — data quality, 利用效果 — utilization outcomes) is constructed, and five methods are applied comprehensively: entropy-weighted TOPSIS, DEA-BCC model, DEMATEL, fuzzy-set Qualitative Comparative Analysis (fsQCA), and multi-period Difference-in-Differences (DID). The study systematically measures, diagnoses, and identifies optimization pathways for provincial OGD platform performance.
The main findings are as follows. First, provincial platform performance exhibits significant regional disparities in a “top-concentrated, bottom-dispersed” pattern. Eastern coastal provinces (Zhejiang, Shanghai, Guangdong, Beijing, Jiangsu) score above 0.75 on the composite index, while the bottom nine provinces score below 0.50. Second, “data caliber illusion” constitutes a systematic bias in existing evaluation frameworks — the median ratio of data catalog entries to valid datasets reaches 8.2:1, indicating that supply-side evaluations based on catalog counts systematically overestimate actual data availability. Third, data quality is the most critical driver of data utilization performance, ranking first in both centrality (4.23) and causality (+1.87) among 12 secondary indicators; its path coefficient to utilization effectiveness (0.47) significantly exceeds that of supply quantity (0.23). Fourth, high-performing platforms follow three equifinal pathways: technology-driven (coverage 0.312), demand-pulled (0.287), and institution-guaranteed (0.198), demonstrating that provinces with different resource endowments can achieve high performance through different routes. Fifth, the policy package accompanying the “Data Twenty Articles” has a significant positive effect on composite performance (+0.043, p<0.01), with the largest effect on 供给保障 (+0.067) and the smallest on 利用效果 (+0.021), indicating a notable time lag in policy impact on utilization outcomes.
Theoretical contributions include: (1) constructing an effectiveness-oriented 4E evaluation framework that shifts the paradigm from supply-oriented to outcome-oriented assessment; (2) discovering and measuring the “data caliber illusion” phenomenon, revealing systematic biases in existing evaluation systems; and (3) combining fsQCA with multi-period DID to form a mixed-methods design of “macro causal identification + micro mechanism revelation,” offering a new methodological paradigm for OGD research. Practical contributions encompass a three-tier policy recommendation system — national institutional restructuring, platform operational upgrading, and regional differentiated pathways — along with a three-phase implementation roadmap for 2026–2030.
Keywords: Open Government Data; Data Utilization Performance; 4E Framework; fsQCA; Multi-period DID; Data Caliber Illusion; Entropy-weighted TOPSIS
